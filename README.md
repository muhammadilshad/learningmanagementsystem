# learningmanagementsystem
E-Learning Web Portal
A web-based learning system designed for the University of Education to help students access online lectures, books, notes, results, and academic news from anywhere. This platform solves the problem of students who cannot attend physical classes by providing complete online learning resources.

**Features**
**For Students**
Watch online video lectures
Download lecture videos, books, notes, and slides
View and download previous study materials
Check latest exam results
Get updated academic news and announcements
Secure login (using university-issued credentials)

**For Admins**
Upload lectures, books, notes, results, and news
Manage subjects, programs, and resources
Control who can access downloaded or streamed content
